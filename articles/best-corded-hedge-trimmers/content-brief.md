## Reader decision

Choose a corded hedge trimmer by matching blade length and stated branch capacity to the hedge, then account for tool weight, handle control, outlet distance, and the cost of a correctly rated outdoor extension cord.

## Search and competitor findings

Current search results mix old corded models with cordless roundups. Useful competitors included Popular Mechanics for the current market shift toward cordless tools, Which? for format and hedge-size distinctions, and Traditional Gardening for a corded-only shortlist. The WhoAdvice opportunity is current US exact-model normalization, dated availability, explicit cord-management costs, and clear separation between hedge trimming and branch pruning.
