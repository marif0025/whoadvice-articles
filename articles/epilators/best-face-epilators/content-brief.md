## Assignment

Help US shoppers choose a purpose-built facial epilator or a currently verified device/attachment approved for facial hair. Resolve the tradeoff between facial precision and the versatility of a larger face-and-body device.

The page must not assume that every body epilator is safe for the face. Treat the exact manufacturer manual as the authority for approved areas such as the upper lip, chin, and cheeks.

## Reader decision

The reader needs to decide:

1. Whether a dedicated facial epilator is preferable to a body device with a facial attachment.
2. Which exact facial areas the device is designed to treat.
3. Whether the head size, controls, power format, and ongoing support justify the price.
4. Whether epilation is a suitable method given discomfort, irritation, or ingrown-hair concerns.
