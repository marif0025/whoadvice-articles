## Reader decision

The reader needs to choose a cordless kit that fits the hedge rather than choosing by voltage alone. The guide must separate blade length from stated cutting capacity, compare loaded weight instead of bare-tool weight where possible, and treat battery and charger contents as part of the purchase.

## Search opportunity

Current results often mix old models, bare tools, kits, and professional machines. WhoAdvice's opportunity is exact-kit normalization, visible evidence limits, loaded-weight comparisons, battery-platform economics, and clear distinctions between routine trimming and pruning thick woody branches.
