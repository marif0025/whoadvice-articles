## Claims contract

### Attribute to the manufacturer

- Every stated cutting capacity or blade gap.
- Every runtime, area-per-charge or charger-speed claim and its available conditions.
- SPM/no-load speed when used to describe operation.
- Weather-resistance, jam/transmission protection and serviceable-gearbox claims.
- Warranty duration and battery-platform compatibility.
- EGO loaded weights, which are editorial calculations from official component weights and must be labeled estimates.

### Do not claim

- That voltage alone measures cutting power or makes one model heavy-duty.
- That a stated capacity proves comfortable routine cutting of equally thick hard, dry or dead wood.
- That any product was tested, handled or used by WhoAdvice.
- Independent runtime, reliability rates, universal comfort or guaranteed balance.
- That review anecdotes prove durability, defects or technical performance.
- That HT2501 is categorically more powerful than HT2601; its award rests on stated capacity and two-speed control.
- A Best Budget winner unless current prices satisfy the approved 20%-below-median rule.
- A single WORX runtime duration while brand answers remain inconsistent.
