## Evidence findings

- CPSC and RYOBI still list RY40620VNM, RY40602VNM, and RY40602BTLVNM in the 2025 hedge-trimmer recall. CPSC still describes unexpected activation and a stop-use remedy.
- BLACK+DECKER's public support record still advises against power-tool sharpening.
- Husqvarna's current US sharpening page still directs users to discard damaged blades and not straighten bent or twisted ones.
- Husqvarna's maintenance page still gives broad guidance of no more than once every 50 accumulated operating hours.
- STIHL's current US guide still supports tool-type power isolation, manual-specific angle selection, forward-only flat filing, lifting on return, limited metal removal, whetstone burr removal, lubrication, approximately 50-hour guidance, and dealer help when uncertain.
- Adjacent citations remain beside recall, powered-tool, damage, service, finishing, and interval claims.
