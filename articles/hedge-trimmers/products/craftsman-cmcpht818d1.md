### Raw specifications and claims

- 18-inch dual-action blade; manufacturer-stated capacity up to 3/4 inch.
- Five-position, 180-degree pivoting head.
- Manufacturer claim: up to 11 ft reach, based on a 6-ft person holding the tool at shoulder height, 85 degrees from the ground, with arms extended. This is not tool length.
- Kit includes one 2.0 Ah V20 battery, charger, and hard blade cover.
- Brushed motor and 3-year limited warranty.
- An authorized-retailer specification lists 7.9 lb, but Popular Mechanics measured/described the tested working tool as just under 11 lb. Preserve this conflict until package and weighing basis are reconciled.

### Independent observations

- Popular Mechanics tested the exact kit in 2026 and reported a 6-ft-8-in overall tool length from rear handle to blade tip.
- Its tester reached an 8-ft shrub, found the five head positions useful for top and side cuts, and described control as good for the format.
- The same test found the fixed pole awkward on small bushes and the cutting action slower than more powerful pole trimmers.
