## Reader decision

Choose a pole hedge trimmer that provides enough usable reach while remaining controllable at the required angle. Tool or shaft length, user-inclusive maximum reach, blade length, stated cut capacity, complete operating weight, shaft design, and package contents must remain separate facts.

## Ranking rationale

### 1. DEWALT DCPH820M1 — Best Overall

The strongest verified specification and package combination in the approved shortlist—a 22-inch blade, 1-inch manufacturer-stated capacity, seven-position head, shoulder strap, and complete 4.0 Ah kit—makes it the most rounded option. Its 12-ft claim is user-inclusive. The absent official working weight and lack of strong exact-model handling evidence prevent any claim that it is the best-proven handling option.

### 2. CRAFTSMAN CMCPHT818D1 — Best Independently Tested Homeowner Option

It has the best current hands-on evidence for the actual buyer decision. Popular Mechanics measured its tested setup at just under 11 lb and a 6-ft-8-in overall length, finding its five head positions and control useful at height while documenting slower cutting and fixed-length awkwardness on lower trimming. Some retailer records list 7.9 lb, but the measurement basis is unclear; do not average the figures or call 7.9 lb confirmed working weight.

### 3. WORX WG252.9 — Best Telescopic Design

WORX provides a clearly defined 7.6-lb weight with battery, plus telescoping adjustment, head articulation, and a rotating handle. It gives up stated capacity and blade speed, and the approved Amazon package is a bare tool. The award is based on adjustment design, not a marginal and inconsistently measured weight comparison.

### 4. Sun Joe SJH904E — Best Corded Convertible

Sun Joe leads the corded subgroup because exact identity, dimensions, weight, warranty, current demand, and publisher destination are documented. Its cable route and 9.5-pound extended load remain the central tradeoffs.

### 5. Earthwise CVPH43018 — Best Documented Corded Value

Earthwise follows closely with an official page and exact manual, 8.8-foot extended length, included strap and cover, and a 0.7-inch stated capacity. Its shorter blade and slightly higher weight place it behind Sun Joe.

### 6. BLACK+DECKER LPHT120B — Best for Light Maintenance

The 7/16-inch stated capacity limits it to regularly maintained, thinner growth. The appeal is a supported 20V MAX platform, five head positions, and a low brand-listed weight whose measurement basis remains unclear. The exact title and table entry must say “BLACK+DECKER LPHT120B — Bare tool; battery and charger not included.” It should never be framed for woody renovation cuts.

### 7. GARCARE GPHT06 — Best Corded Adjustment Range

GARCARE offers six head positions, a rotating rear handle, and convertible reach, but unresolved exact working weight, manual, and warranty evidence reduce its score. It remains useful when adjustment range matters and the cable route is simple.

### Specialist: Husqvarna 122LKH — Best Gas Alternative

The exact model has a 21.6cc two-cycle engine, 21-inch blade, 0.75-inch manufacturer-stated capacity, 89.72-inch product length, and approximately 15-lb weight. Bob Vila supports only the attributed test observations about overhead reach, strap use, cutting the tested material, and fatigue. Its ownership demands make it relevant for repeated or larger workloads, not the average homeowner seeking occasional tall-hedge access.

## Required Part 2 buying-guide coverage

- Balance and weight distribution: explain why a lighter tool may still feel difficult when weight is concentrated near the cutting head.
- Collapsed and extended length: compare storage, transport, lower-face work, and tall-hedge access where exact figures exist.
- Fixed versus telescopic compromise: telescopic adjustment can improve fit across heights but may flex or become cumbersome at full extension.
- Head angle and locking system: position count alone does not establish adjustment ease or locking security.
- Bare-tool working cost: include the compatible battery and charger required for WORX WG252.9 and BLACK+DECKER LPHT120B.
- Overhead debris and power-line safety: direct readers to exact manuals and PPE requirements; do not improvise safe-clearance instructions.
- Pole trimmer versus pruning tool: stated tooth capacity is not permission to repeatedly cut woody limbs at the maximum diameter.
