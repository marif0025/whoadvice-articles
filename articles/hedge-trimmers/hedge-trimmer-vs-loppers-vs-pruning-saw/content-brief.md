## Reader decision

The reader needs to distinguish bulk trimming from selective pruning. A hedge trimmer removes many small outer stems quickly to shape a hedge. Loppers use leverage for individual branches within their documented capacity. A pruning saw handles larger woody branches that require a sawing cut. The article must avoid universal diameter promises because species, wood condition, tool design, and manufacturer limits vary.

## Opening angle

Lead with the work rather than the tools. Use a hedge trimmer for repeated outer growth and shaping, loppers for individual stems that fit safely between the jaws, and a pruning saw for larger woody branches requiring controlled selective cuts. Mixed hedges may require more than one tool.
