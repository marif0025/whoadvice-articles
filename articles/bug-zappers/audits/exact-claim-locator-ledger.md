## Classification rules

- **Supported:** current evidence directly supports the same mechanism, insect, setting, and endpoint.
- **Needs qualification:** a narrower version may be supportable after context and limits are added.
- **Product-specific only:** may appear only after exact model/manual or exact-system evidence.
- **Unsupported:** no adequate current evidence for the statement as written.
- **Remove:** the statement is materially misleading, absolute, or high-risk and should not survive a rewrite without new direct evidence.

“Exact claim” below is a short verbatim locator, not an approved quotation for future public copy. Product-level specifications outside these high-risk categories remain subject to later exact-model verification.
