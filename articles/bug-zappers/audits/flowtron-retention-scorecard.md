## Outcome logic

- **Retain:** independent demand or business value plus a distinct comparison role.
- **Merge:** weak independent value plus substantial overlap with outdoor, after redirect/link-equity planning.
- **Hold:** performance evidence unavailable or current model/decision scope unresolved.

No single signal automatically decides retention.

## Current decision

**Hold.** Product depth alone is not enough to retain the page. Search demand, link equity, and revenue are unavailable, while the current comparison job substantially overlaps the outdoor page and crosses into indoor/commercial intent.

Do not redirect, merge, rewrite, or approve the page. If later retained, its contract must define whether it is an outdoor residential model-family comparison or another distinct job; it cannot continue as an all-format Flowtron catalogue.

## Data request for final decision

1. Search Console export by page and query for at least the last 16 months, including the Flowtron, outdoor, and pillar URLs.
2. Backlink/referring-page export for the Flowtron URL.
3. Affiliate click, conversion, and revenue evidence attributable to the page.
4. Exact current Flowtron lineup with official manuals and US package status.
5. Fresh SERP capture for branded and comparison queries at final review.
6. Proposed unique reader decision and a no-overlap shortlist boundary.

## If merge later becomes justified

Before approval, prepare—but do not implement—a target mapping, one-hop 301 plan, canonical update, internal-link replacement list, backlink preservation review, affiliate-destination migration, and post-launch monitoring plan.
