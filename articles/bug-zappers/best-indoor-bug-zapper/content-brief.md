## Reader decision

Help the reader choose an indoor-rated stationary device for a defined flying-insect problem and room. The selected set requires a clear choice between a UV glue-board trap and explicitly indoor-rated guarded electric grids without treating every small fly as a “gnat.”

## Claim and evidence controls

- Some indoor UV traps may capture some flies; species, setting, competing light, and placement matter.
- Sticky-card capture of adult fungus gnats does not resolve moist potting media.
- Drain/moth and fruit-fly problems require breeding-source correction.
- Do not place a high-voltage electrocution trap near exposed food or food-preparation areas; exact public wording must remain tied to applicable evidence/manuals.
- Do not infer general safety from enclosure, guarding, or the absence of insecticide.
