## Eligibility and ranking method

Products were screened in this order: exact model/package, explicit indoor permission, mechanism clarity, exact instructions, current US route, servicing/consumables, identity conflicts, and CPSC recall result. A failure in identity, location, instructions, recall, or current availability cannot be offset by advertised voltage, coverage, popularity, or review stars.

Private comparison weights for the later contract: exact-product confidence 20%; room/location fit 20%; mechanism and insect-job fit 15%; ownership burden 15%; placement/food-area evidence 10%; power/noise evidence 10%; current availability 5%; evidence quality 5%.

## Product findings

### Safer Home SH502 — approved

- Strongest current indoor identity: exact SH502 one-device/two-card package, compatible SH503/SH506/SH512 cards, current brand and Amazon routes.
- It is a sticky trap, not an electrified zapper. Public copy must disclose this immediately.
- Primary drawback: recurring glue-card cost and no evidence that trapping some adults removes an indoor breeding source.

### SereneLife/Pyle PSLBZ25 — approved for comparison

- Exact official page and manual support plug-in UV/fan capture and indoor/outdoor positioning.
- It offers a reusable chamber instead of glue-board replacement, but introduces fan noise and cleaning questions that must stay tied to the manual or measured owner evidence.
- Do not turn the IPX4 claim into blanket weatherproof or safety wording.

### BLACK+DECKER BDPC-970 — conditional

- Official exact page gives a stable model, GTIN, one-unit/two-tube package, and glue-tube mechanism.
- Official fields are incomplete or contradictory: `cordless` without battery details and indoor positioning alongside protected-outdoor copy.
- Needs the exact manual plus a current purchase route before recommendation.

### Flowtron FLWIFAN10 — rejected for recommendation

- Current exact manufacturer page establishes USB-C fan capture and removable chamber.
- Direct stock was sold out and no exact manual or independent route was located.
- The one available brand-site review reported no captures; this is a warning signal, not sufficient proof of general ineffectiveness.

### Flowtron FLWFPLW — conditional

- Exact white model and compatible replacement-board pack are normalized; do not merge it with black FLWFPLB.
- Direct unit was sold out and exact instructions were not archived.

### Flowtron FLWINZ11 — conditional

- The official variant page maps the 12W/two-6W-bulb unit to FLWINZ11 and states indoor hanging/table use.
- All page variants were sold out. No exact manual/current independent route was located.
- Advertised 3,000V, 300-square-foot coverage, and family-safety language are not performance or safety findings.

## SERP and reader-decision findings

Current broad editorial pages mix glue traps, fan traps, and grids and often use coverage or voltage as a shortcut for performance. WhoAdvice should instead lead with the insect/source, room, capture method, competing light, noise tolerance, and maintenance cost. The article must route outdoor, solar, and active-racket intent to their own pages.

## Recall and claim controls

- Exact-model/category CPSC searches on July 22, 2026 located no matching recall for these six candidates. This is an internal search finding, not a `recall-free` claim.
- CPSC recall 25-396 for iMirror BZ-001 remains an exclusion example, not evidence about these products.
- No universal child/pet safety, food-area suitability, silence, non-toxic, coverage, mosquito, or elimination claim is approved.
- Adult capture does not establish source elimination for fungus gnats, drain flies, or fruit flies.
