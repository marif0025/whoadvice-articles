## Eligibility and ranking method

Required gates: exact model, true solar power, exact charge/battery instructions, outdoor placement/weather limits, cleaning/storage, current US route, identity conflicts, and CPSC result. Decorative light, advertised runtime, panel size, coverage, voltage, or retailer stars cannot rescue a missing manual or route.

Private later-contract weights: exact identity/manual 20%; solar charging practicality 20%; current US availability 15%; placement/weather evidence 15%; battery/runtime evidence 10%; ownership/cleaning/storage 10%; mechanism clarity 5%; evidence quality 5%.

The article's reader decision is whether cordless placement is worth dependence on direct sunlight, battery capacity, shorter/variable runtime, and more uncertain replacement support than a corded outdoor grid.

## Recall and claim controls

- Exact-model/category CPSC searches on July 22, 2026 found no matching recall for these five products; do not say `recall-free`.
- Solar charging does not prove all-night operation. Runtime changes with sunlight, battery condition, temperature, selected light mode, and device load.
- Weather-resistant/IP claims apply only to the exact model and do not mean immersion-safe or maintenance-free.
- UV/grid kills do not establish reduced mosquito biting or disease risk.
