## Eligibility and ranking method

The criteria were fixed before the contract:

| Criterion | Weight | What counts |
|---|---:|---|
| Exact identity and instructions | 20% | Exact model/package match, manual provenance, and conflict burden |
| Controls and guarding | 20% | Activation behavior, switch or lock logic, grid access, and documented warnings |
| Current US availability | 15% | Current Amazon route, selected variation integrity, and publisher confirmation |
| Power practicality | 15% | Rechargeable versus replaceable batteries, charging method, and indicators |
| Cleaning and storage evidence | 10% | Exact shutdown, discharge, cleaning, and storage directions |
| Handling evidence | 10% | Verified dimensions, weight, folding design, or other decision-relevant handling facts |
| Ownership pattern | 5% | Recurring, variant-matched feedback with limitations recorded |
| Overall evidence quality | 5% | Primary-source depth and unresolved conflicts |

Advertised voltage, spark intensity, sound, target-insect lists, and visible kills are not performance scores. Unknown fields receive no neutral or assumed credit.
