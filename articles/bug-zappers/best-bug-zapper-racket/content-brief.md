## Claim and evidence controls

- A racket can kill an insect contacted by an energized grid; that does not establish fewer insects in the room or yard.
- A separately verified base/light mode needs its own placement and expectation language.
- Guarding reduces access to a grid only to the extent documented; it is not proof of universal child or pet safety.
- Do not infer effectiveness from voltage, spark intensity, sound, or visible contact alone.
- Keep cleaning and discharge directions exact to the approved manual.
