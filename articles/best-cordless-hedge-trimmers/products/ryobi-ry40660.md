### Raw specifications and claims

- 26-inch blade; 1.5-inch stated cut capacity; 2,800–3,200 SPM; two speeds; rotating handle; anti-jam; magnesium gearbox.
- 11.7 lb stated weight; brand runtime claim up to 29 minutes with included 2Ah battery.
- Five-year tool and three-year battery warranties.

### Safety/recall

- CPSC's June 12, 2025 recall applies to 24-inch RY40620VNM, RY40602VNM and RY40602BTLVNM within specified serial ranges, not RY40660/RY40606: https://www.cpsc.gov/Recalls/2025/TTI-Outdoor-Power-Equipment-Recalls-RYOBI-40-Volt-24-Inch-Cordless-Hedge-Trimmers-Due-to-Laceration-Hazard

- Highest stated capacity candidate for genuinely thick hedge growth, plus anti-jam and two speeds.
- Tradeoffs are substantial weight, short claimed runtime for the kit battery, high kit price, and no Amazon eligibility.
